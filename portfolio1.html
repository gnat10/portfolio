PDF to Audio Converter

A program that convert pdf files to mp3 audio files. When I read books, I feel guilty because I didn't do anything productive. This program helps me so that I can listen to story while doing something else.

The challenge was to have a program that can also adjust the audio settings such as speed, voice and volume. Another part is to have a GUI that is easy to understand and use.
